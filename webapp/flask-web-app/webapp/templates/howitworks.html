What is Information Retrieval?

Information Retrieval is a research field that that deals with the process of obtaining resources which are relevant to an information need. When you are saying "Hey Google, can you tell me what's the weather like in Rome?" you are making use of Information Retrieval systems in order to retrieve the information you need.

Quantum Annealing... the future?

Quantum Annealing is a Quantum Computing paradigm that can be used to solve optimization problems such as the Travelling Salesman. It can be very powerful since it can be applied to solve NP-Hard problems that are infeasible to be solved by means of classical hardware.

How does QuantumCLEF work?

QuantumCLEF is an evaluation campaign consisting of several Shared-Tasks. You can participate with your own research group for free in one of the task by registering your group. You will propose your own solution and a paper reporting what you have done and achieved. At the end, all the groups' result will be reported and if your paper is well-written it will be published.
